Site Name

Wired Creations - One of a Kind Jewelry for the Ones You Love

Site Purpose with Goals

The purpose of this site is to showcase handcrafted jewelry. The site will get the visitor excited about being in and exploring the wonders of nature. The goals of the website are to:

  • Provide the visitor with examples of the craftmanship and creativity Amanda is capable of producing.
  • Showcase completed jewelry pieces that have been sold or are available for sale.
  • Provide a contact form to reach Amanda about a potential project.

Target Audience and Scenarios

Target Audiences

The target audiences include the following cohorts:

  • Individiuals looking for unique jewelry pieces to complement their daily wear.
  • Indiivduals looking for a jewelry piece to wear on a special occassion (e.g. birthday or celebration).
  • Individuals looking for a gift for someone else.

Scenarios

Customers should be able to accomplish the identified scenarios below.

  • Explore the completed jewelry pieces, and reach out to Amanda for purchase if the piece is available.
  • Use the contact form to inquire about a potential future project/creation.
  • Use a filter to view only those pieces the visitor is interested in (e.g. earrings, or pendants).

Page Content

Three main topics/webpages:

  • Introduction: What makes Wired Creations special? (Home)
  • Showcase Completed Pieces (Gallery)
  • Contact Amanda if you need customization. (Contact Us Form)

Introductions:

Who am I? I'm a mother, a wife, and a crafter. I have dabbled in various crafting mediums throughout my life…cake decorating, crocheting, painting, and now wire and stone. I love how each of these crafting mediums have allowed me to explore “imperfection”. Most of my life I have felt that I needed to reach a self-imposed level of perfectionism, only to fail. Through crafting, I have been able to hold on to my attention to detail while letting go of my idea of perfect. Each time I craft a pair of earrings or a pendant, I may start out with one idea of how the piece should look but I find the finished product looks so much better.

Gallery:

I want to create a portfolio of my completed pieces to share with others the beauty I have been able to create. If time permits, I would like the visitor to be able to filter the gallery by the type of piece they are looking for (e.g. earrings or pendant).

Contact Us form:

Other than standard (Name, Email, and message) - I want the visitor to indicate their reason for contact me (either to ask a question, or to purchase a piece).
