Site Name

Dry Oar Witewater Rafting Expedition Company - The premier experience of our local river and surrounding areas.

Site Purpose with Goals

The purpose of this site is to showcase our local river and the variety of activities that are available for a day trip or a weekend adventure. The site will get the visitor excited about being in and exploring the wonders of nature. Our goals of the website are to:

  • Provide the visitor with a variety of facts about our local river and the surrounding areas.
  • Help the visitor plan their visit with a variety of activities.
  • Encourage younger children to want to be outdoors.

Target Audience and Scenarios

Target Audiences

The target audiences include the following cohorts:

  • Individiuals looking to get away from the hussle and bussle of life.
  • Schools looking for a close up view of nature for their students.
  • The family looking to spend more quality time together without the regular distractions of electronics.
  • Groups wanting an outside venue for their retreat or team building events.

Scenarios

Customers should be able to accomplish the identified scenarios below.

  • Explore the activities that are available by the river, like fishing or swimming, based on the time of the year.
  • Look up the anticipated weather for a specific time of year. Is the weather ideal for the activities they are intersted in?
  • Make a reservation with a whitewater rafting guide.
  • View the nature hiking trails available, by length of the trail.
  • View online activities the kids can complete - match the shadow with the animal, a simple maze to help the lost camper find their tent, or download a coloring sheet.
